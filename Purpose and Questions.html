Project Purpose

We used this project to try and gain some insight into the different areas of the Chicagoland area. By analyzing the demographics of the different areas we looked to see how they reflected on the public school system. This research may be helpful for someone looking into living in the city, especially if they have kids who will have to attend a public school.

Questions We Looked to Answer

  • Where are most of the public schools located in Chicago?
  • How do poverty rates correlate with environment scores across zip codes?
  • How does poverty rate correlate with college enrollment?
  • How does the racial composition of an area relate to how the school is perceived?
  • Is there a correlation between rates of single-parent households and rate of misconduct?
  • Are there more schools in wealthy areas?
  • Do schools in poorer districts perform worse?
